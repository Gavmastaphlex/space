ABOUT US

SPACE Architecture Studio is an award winning architecture and interior design practice located in Mana, Wellington and Waikanae on the Kapiti Coast. The studio was established in 2001 by the current directors Tushka Glintmeyer and Nathan Rooney.

We are a team of Architectural Designers, Registered Architects, and Interior Design specialists. Our design portfolio includes a wide range of residential and commercial work with a focus on quality sustainable buildings.

OUR PORTFOLIO

Our portfolio of work includes:

  • New Homes.
  • Residential Alterations and Refurbishments.
  • Apartment Buildings and Multi-unit Developments.
  • Commercial New Buildings and Alterations.
  • Aged Care Hospitals and Retirement Complexes.
  • Residential Interior Design.
  • Commercial & Corporate Interior Design & Fit-out.
  • Hospitality Design and Fit-out.

We see each project as an opportunity to create a tailored design solution and strive for excellence throughout all aspects of our work.

Our projects feature throughout New Zealand. We value the opportunity to be involved in exciting projects wherever they may be located.

SPACE PHILOSOPHY

We believe that our surroundings directly influence the quality of our lives, whether in the work place, at home or in the spaces between – our environments have a direct impact on our well-being.

We approach all of our projects with this philosophy in mind – our design response is always considerate of the unique context of the project, the landscape and environment, and sensitive to our client's individual requirements.

DESIGN ETHOS

Our design ethos is "architecture without ego". All of our project outcomes are driven by our clients requirements and objectives, not our own. We work with our clients in a collaborative effort to ensure positive outcomes. Our aim is to achieve innovative, responsible solutions on budget. We are not only concerned with aesthetic merit, but are also concerned with ensuring our buildings perform well, are suitable for the end user and add value to the lives of their inhabitants and are a pleasure to work or live in.
